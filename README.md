# Pizza-Sales-Dashboard

Short Description

A Power BI dashboard that visualizes pizza sales performance across weekdays, months, categories, pizza sizes, top-selling items, and hourly demand. It provides a complete overview of revenue, total orders, and quantity sold.

Business Problem

Difficulty in understanding which days, pizza types, and sizes drive the highest sales, making it challenging to optimize inventory, staffing, and menu strategy.

Goal of the Dashboard

To provide a unified, interactive view of pizza sales trends that helps track revenue, identify best-selling products, analyze customer demand patterns, and support data-driven operational decisions.

Business Impact

- Supports better inventory management by revealing high-demand days and hours.
- Helps improve profit margins by identifying top revenue-generating pizza categories and sizes.
- Enhances staffing efficiency by showing peak-hour demand.
- Informs menu optimization by highlighting best-selling and underperforming pizzas.

Key Insights

- Friday shows the highest revenue, indicating strong end-week demand.
- Large (L) and Medium (M) sizes contribute the most to total quantity sold.
- Classic and Supreme categories generate the highest revenue overall.
- Top-selling pizzas include The Classic Deluxe and Barbecue Chicken Pizza.

- Monthly revenue remains consistent with slight peaks in July and November.
